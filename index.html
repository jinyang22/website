Sculpting in Time, 1984

______________________________________

Some fifteen years ago, as I was jotting down notes for the first draft of this
book, I found myself wondering whether there really was any point in writing it at all.

Why not just go on making one film after another, finding practical solutions
to those theoretical problems which arise whenever one is working on a film?
